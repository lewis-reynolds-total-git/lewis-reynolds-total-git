About Jim's


At Lewis's we LOVE coffee! Whether you are looking for that perfect coffee, wanting to learn to make your own, or in need of supplies for your existing buddy; we are your one-stop-shop! We have everything from the tiniest of tanks to the biggest of breeding set-ups. We also carry a full line of foods, both live and pellets, as well as all the necessary supplements.

Our mission is to provide quality care for all coffee, whether they are in our store or in your home. We also strongly believe in coffee rescue and have coffee available for sale.

Turtle Rescue
